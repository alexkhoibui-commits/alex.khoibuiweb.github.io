About Me

I am a student at Le Hong Phong High School for the Gifted, a prestigious school known for its strong academic tradition. In grade 10, I earned a GPA of 9.46, and with consistent effort and clear direction, I am actively working toward a GPA of 9.6 in grade 11.


To enhance my performance, I utilize modern tools like Notebook LM to summarize and visualize knowledge. Engineering & Projects

Aerospace Engineering

My Journey Toward the Sky

My biggest dream is to study aerospace engineering at a university in the United States. I am currently learning how to build and control drones at UTE University. Every hour spent with circuits, motors, and flight controllers helps me build the foundation of my future in the science of flight. Balancing this passion with a demanding high school workload is challenging, but every mistake and success brings me closer to my goal.

Technology & AI

Drones and AI: Drowning Prevention

In Vietnam, unusual whirlpools and deep pools at beaches can lead to tragic drowning accidents. To solve this, I am researching how drones and Artificial Intelligence can increase visibility for lifeguards and process live video to detect signs of drowning. This combination could reduce response times and save lives, allowing pupils to relax safely during summer breaks.

Leadership & Social Impact

Leadership

I proudly serve as a three-year classmaster and the leader of the STEM & Career Guidance Club. These roles have allowed me to develop strong communication and organizational skills while guiding my peers.

Community Service

Despite my busy schedule, I contribute to the "love kitchen" at Cho Ray Hospital by giving out instant noodles, soap, paper towels, and rice. Knowing that most patients are seriously ill and face heavy financial burdens, I hope my small acts of kindness show them that society has not forgotten them during their hardships.

Martial Arts & Discipline

Training alongside professional fighters motivates me to push my physical and mental limits.
